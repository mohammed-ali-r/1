Our Animals

Step into the realm of Wild World Zoo and encounter Humphrey, our magnificent hippopotamus. Humphrey has been a cherished inhabitant of our zoo for over a decade, delighting visitors with his playful antics and endearing charm. Known for his laid-back demeanor and love for lounging in the sun, Humphrey has become an iconic figure at Wild World. With his massive yet gentle presence, Humphrey embodies the spirit of the African savannah, captivating hearts and leaving lasting memories for all who visit our zoo.
